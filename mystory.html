My story

Chalew Deghebo

This is my developer story.


  • As a child, there was no computer or even cell-phone in the family.

  • In the high School, we had few computers for Computer Lab class for the whole school, and I had only two Lab classes the whole school year to learn;
    turning-on and off a PC, how to search on an Internet Explorer, how to use Microsoft word and excel, and saving files.

  • In the college, I had a better access to computers at the college's library where I used to use computers to gather more information when I had assignments.

  • After I had arrived in the USA, I had better access to the Internet and computers in the Public library.

  • I always had an interest and love for technology.
    The first P.C I bought was called called Toshiba-Satellite.

  • Currently, I joined Refcode community with the 2022-Spring-cohort training to learn more about Software Engineering where my dream is always being a Software Engineer.
